Completed on June 2nd

The Belle Creek Community hosted a garage sale on June 2nd starting. There was dozens of different homes selling everything from children's toys and clothes to books and tools.

Many of the homes chose to sell in the park and these homes saw a lot more traffic than many of the others. The bouncy castle was a big hit with kids. The Metro District would like to thank everyone who participated in the sale or came to buy. This year was a big success and we hope to build on this in the future.

Some areas that we plan to improve next year include:

  • Put large banners at the entrances to the community.
  • Again offer, but also advertise, free-to-use tables in the park.
  • We discovered that donation centers will not pick up on the weekends - look into providing a drop off zone at the family center for donations to be picked up during the week.
  • Look into extending the hours/days.
